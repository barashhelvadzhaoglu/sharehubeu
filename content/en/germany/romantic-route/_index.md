Germany's most famous tourist route — the Romantische Straße, stretching 460 km from Würzburg to Füssen.

We didn't drive it. We took the **Deutschlandticket** — with two kids aged 1 and 5, and a double stroller. Medieval town centres, cobblestones, castle climbs — and what all of that looks like when you're pushing a pram.

No tourist brochure language here. Just first-hand experience and practical information.
