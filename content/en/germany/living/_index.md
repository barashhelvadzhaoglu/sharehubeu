Moving to Germany is a decision — but the decision never really ends.

Driving licence, rental contracts, labour court, Jobcenter, health insurance, childcare... I've been through all of it. I compared it to Turkey, found some things misleading, discovered unexpected advantages.

This section covers life in Germany as it actually is — not through official documents, but through the notes of someone living it.
