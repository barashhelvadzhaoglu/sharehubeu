## Füssen: The City Living in Neuschwanstein's Shadow

Everyone comes to Füssen for Neuschwanstein — but leaves without seeing Füssen itself. This article is for the city: Hohes Schloss, the Altstadt, the Lech River and surroundings.

Castle guide separately: [Neuschwanstein Castle | Disney's Inspiration →](/en/germany/neuschwanstein-castle-guide/)

---

## Getting There

**Train:** Direct Regional Express from Munich Hauptbahnhof. ~2 hours. Deutschlandticket valid.

---

## Places to Visit

### 1. Hohes Schloss (High Castle)
On the hill overlooking the city. The most important structure in Füssen for those not going to Neuschwanstein. 15th-century summer residence of Bavarian princes.

**Fascinating detail:** For someone looking from outside, you can't tell the windows on the walls aren't real — all windows are painted using trompe l'œil (optical illusion) technique. Very impressive.

Art gallery inside (Staatsgalerie). Entry ~6 EUR. Outside free.

### 2. Altstadt (Old Town)
Right below Hohes Schloss. Colorful facade houses, narrow streets, historic buildings. Better preserved than most Bavarian small towns.

### 3. Lech River Canyon (Lechhöhlen)
The Lech River running right beside the city has created a small canyon here. Looking down from the bridge, the turquoise water and rocks are incredible. Free, easy access.

### 4. Forggensee (Lake Forggen)
Artificial lake 2 km south of Füssen. Great for swimming in summer. Lakeside cycling and walking paths. Alps and Neuschwanstein in the background — ideal photo spot.

**Tip:** The best angle to photograph Neuschwanstein is from Forggensee.

---

## Practical Information

**How long?** 2–3 hours for the city. Add Forggensee or Neuschwanstein for a full day.

**Stroller:** Altstadt partly cobblestone but manageable. Forggensee lakeside excellent.

---

## FAQ

**Can Füssen town be visited independently of Neuschwanstein?** Absolutely. 2–3 beautiful hours just for the city.

**Is Forggensee close to Füssen?** Yes, ~2 km. Easy by foot or bike.

**Is an overnight stay needed in Füssen?** Not for just the city. A full day covers Neuschwanstein + town + Forggensee.
