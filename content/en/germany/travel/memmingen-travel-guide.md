## Memmingen: Bavaria's Hidden Medieval Gem

When people think of medieval Bavaria, Rothenburg ob der Tauber comes to mind. But there's a far less touristy, far more authentic alternative: **Memmingen**.

The city walls are almost entirely intact. Historic buildings surrounding the Marktplatz have kept their original character. No tourist crowds — so you can actually feel the city.

From Munich by Deutschlandticket: **1.5 hours, free**.

---

## Getting There

**Train:** Regional Express direct from Munich Hauptbahnhof. About 1 hour 20–30 minutes. Deutschlandticket valid.

**Station to city center:** 10–15 minutes on foot. Flat and easy.

---

## Places to Visit

### 1. Marktplatz
The heart of the city. A wide square surrounded by historic buildings. In the center is a fountain, alongside the **Steuerhaus** (tax house) — dating from the 15th century, the city's most beautiful building with incredible carved facade details.

A weekly market is held here with local products, fruit, vegetables and cheese.

### 2. Stadtmauer (City Walls)
What makes Memmingen special: the walls are largely still standing. You can walk along them and look out over the city from the towers. Total walk ~45 minutes, not strenuous.

### 3. Martinskirche (St. Martin's Church)
Right next to the square. Late Gothic style, dating from the 14th century. Interior is quite impressive — especially the wooden carved choir section. Free entry.

### 4. Sieben Dächer (Seven Roofs) District
Memmingen's most photographed spot. Seven wooden structures leaning against each other over a small canal. Medieval, once used by leather craftsmen. Now fully restored, very photogenic.

### 5. Weberhaus (Weavers' House)
On the corner of Marktplatz, 16th-century home of the weavers' guild. Frescoes still vivid.

---

## Practical Information

**How long?** 3–4 hours is sufficient. Leave after breakfast, return by afternoon.

**Stroller:** City center is cobblestone. Some wall sections tricky. Center is manageable but not perfect.

**Best time:** Weekdays — weekends with the market are nice too.

---

## FAQ

**How to get to Memmingen from Munich?** Regional Express direct, ~1.5 hours. Deutschlandticket valid.

**How long to spend in Memmingen?** 3–4 hours for the city. Add Allgäu region for a full day.

**Is Memmingen expensive?** Much more affordable than Munich. Small city, no tourist prices.
