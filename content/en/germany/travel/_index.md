One of the best things about living in Munich: there are incredible cities in every direction within an hour.

We did all of these trips with a **Deutschlandticket**, **two kids aged 1 and 5**, and a **double stroller**. Which cities are stroller-friendly? Which museums can you get into, which castles can't you climb? Where to eat, how long is enough?

All of that here — not a tourist brochure, but real experience.
